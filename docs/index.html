1 Preface

[Edit on GDocs]

This is a manual for the contributors and editorial team of the Citizen Science Guide for Research Libraries from the LIBER Citizen Science Working Group.

The manual is made of three parts:

  • A Style Guide Manual: For contributors, reviewers, editors
  • An Editorial Manual: For editors and reviewers
  • A Publishing Manual: For the publishing and platform workflows
